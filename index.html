About AMARDEEP STEEL CENTRE

Incorporated in year 1984, Amardeep Steel Centre is a well known dealer, supplier and exporter of raw materials like pipes, tubes, plates, fittings, fasteners etc. as well as process equipment like heat exchanger, pressure vessel, reactor, columns, towers, tube-bundles, etc made up of Tantalum, Titanium, Zirconium, Hastelloy, Niobium, Inconel, Monel, Nickel Alloys, Duplex Steel, Stainless Steel, Alloy Steel, Carbon Steel, Schedule 40 & 80 etc.
